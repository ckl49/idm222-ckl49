Frequently Asked Questions

What is Slather?

Slather is a milky body serum that exfoliates textured skin including KP, dry patches, bumps, strawberry skin and flakiness for soft touch skin.

Good for sensitive skin?

Good for sensitive skin?

Where does Slather fit into my routine?

We recommend applying Slather after a warm shower. If using the Soft Touch Body Duo, we recommend a routine like this: Like Butter Mist –> Slather Body Serum –> Body Lotion (optional).

What does Slather do?

Slather helps to moisturize and exfoliate dry patches related to follicle-related chronic skin conditions including KP, bumps, strawberry skin, and flakiness for soft touch skin.

When does Slather expire?

Slather has a shelf life of 24-36 months!

Where can I use Slather?

Slather can be used all over the body; however, we do not recommend using Slather on your face!

What should I not use with Slather?

Slather already contains tons of great ingredients like Urea and Retinol so we do not recommend using Slather in the same routine with AHAs, BHAs or retinol. If you would like to use other actives, it’s best to use it on alternating days.

Can I use Slather if I am pregnant women, trying to get pregnant, or breastfeeding?

No, we do not recommend using Slather if you are pregnant because Slather contains retinol.

Is Slather vegan or gluten free?

Yes, Slather is vegan, nut, and dairy free!

Can I refrigerate my Slather?

Yes, Slather can be put in the fridge!
