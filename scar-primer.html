How To Use

To Prime Before Makeup: After applying moisturizer and sunscreen, dab a small amount of Sealed onto pitted scars or enlarged pores. Let product sit for 30 seconds before applying makeup.

To Wear Without Makeup: Dab a small amount into pitted scars or enlarged pores. Let product sit for 30 seconds, then follow with moisturizer and sunscreen.

Pro Tip: Do not massage in product, it is intended to remain on the surface in order to fill in and smooth uneven texture. For best results, use daily.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any products I should avoid while using Sealed?
Sealed can be applied in conjunction with other active ingredients, but it is important to ensure that the previous layer has been completely absorbed and dried. Experiment with different layering techniques to determine the most effective method for your skin. Remember not to massage the product to prevent pilling. Additionally, it is crucial to always follow up with an SPF product for sun protection.
This has retinol, can I use it during the daytime?
While this specific retinol product does not increase photosensitivity in the skin, we highly recommend incorporating daily sunscreen usage into your skincare routine as a proactive measure to protect your skin from harmful UV rays.
What’s the difference between Sealed and Faded?
Faded = dark pigmented scars. Sealed = 3 dimensional, indented scars, no dark brown/black/grey pigment. Faded diminishes the appearance of persistent discoloration, marks left after blemishes, and scars, whereas Sealed targets the treatment of indented scars and large pores by instantly filling in the indentations while gradually improving skin texture over time.
Can this be used all over the body, not just the face?
Absolutely! Sealed is designed to be your go-to solution for atrophic scarring concerns, whether it's on your face, body, or anywhere else you wish to treat. Our versatile formula empowers you to embrace a holistic approach to scar care, ensuring that you receive the utmost effectiveness and results across all areas of your skin.
How long will it take for me to see results?
You will see an instant cosmetic improvement in the appearance of indented scars and large pores. Over time, the appearance of scar depth is improved, often around 8 weeks of daily use.
Will this work for surgical scars?
We haven't conducted specific tests on the Sealed product formula in relation to surgical scarring. While Sealed is formulated to address a wide range of scarring concerns, including atrophic scarring, we have not specifically evaluated its efficacy on surgical scars.
Will this help flatten hypertrophic scars?
We haven't conducted specific tests on the effectiveness of Sealed in addressing hypertrophic scars. While Sealed is formulated to target a broad range of scarring concerns, including atrophic scarring, we have not specifically evaluated its impact on hypertrophic scars.
Is Sealed suitable for all skin types?
Sealed is formulated to be suitable for all skin types. Whether you have dry, oily, sensitive, or combination skin, you can confidently incorporate Sealed into your skincare routine.
When does Sealed expire?
