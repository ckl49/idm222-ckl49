What’s the difference between Sealed and Faded?
Faded = dark pigmented scars. Sealed = 3 dimensional, indented scars, no dark brown/black/grey pigment. Faded diminishes the appearance of persistent discoloration, marks left after blemishes, and scars, whereas Sealed targets the treatment of indented scars and large pores by instantly filling in the indentations while gradually improving skin texture over time.

Absolutely! Sealed is designed to be your go-to solution for atrophic scarring concerns, whether it's on your face, body, or anywhere else you wish to treat. Our versatile formula empowers you to embrace a holistic approach to scar care, ensuring that you receive the utmost effectiveness and results across all areas of your skin.
