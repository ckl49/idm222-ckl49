Frequently Asked Questions

Why did we reformulate Like Butter Mask?

Like Butter has graduated from a hydrating mask to a turbo-charged daily moisturizer with OTC-level 1% colloidal oatmeal to alleviate eczema symptoms, moisturize and soothe dry skin.

The modified texture is richer and creamier, making it easier to incorporate into your daily skincare regimen. Active ingredients support the skin barrier that helps skin defend itself from environmental irritants.
