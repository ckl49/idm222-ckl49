Why did we reformulate Like Butter Mask?

The modified texture is richer and creamier, making it easier to incorporate into your daily skincare regimen. Why steroid-free?
Using steroids to treat eczema over a long period of time can lead to side effects like thinning skin, stretch marks, and increased susceptibility to infections. Break the cycle of dependence today. At Topicals, we’re dedicated to creating skincare that’s safe for long-term — for every skin type, shade, and age.
Is it an eczema treatment or a moisturizer?
Both! Like Butter is a daily rich moisturizer that doubles as an over the counter eczema treatment. Because it’s steroid-free, it’s safe for daily use as a rich, hydrating moisturizer head-to-toe.
So it has oatmeal, what's the big deal?
Like Butter is an OTC grade moisturizer that contains 1% colloidal oatmeal which is approved, tested and proven by the FDA to relieve eczema symptoms.
Does Like Butter expire?
Because Like Butter is an over-the-counter product with 1% colloidal oatmeal, we are required to list an expiration date on the packaging. Using the product after the expiration date will not guarantee results from the active ingredients, however it is perfectly safe to use.
