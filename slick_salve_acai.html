Frequently Asked Questions

What does Slick Salve Acai smell like?
Slick Salve Acai has a very subtle fruity scent, but is fragrance free.
Is this tinted? What does it look like on skin?
Slick Salve Acai delivers a rich but buildable berry tint and a high-shine, glossy finish that leaves your lips looking ultra-slick and deeply hydrated.
Can I layer this with the clear version?
Both products offer a beautiful glossy finish, but for the best long-lasting wear, we recommend enjoying them individually to let each formula shine on its own.
Will this break me out?
There are no ingredients in this product that should lead to breakouts. If you are experiencing this, please discontinue usage.
Does this plump lips?
No, Slick Salve does not plump the lips. However, it provides deep hydration that may lead to the appearance of fuller lips.
Is this product flattering on all skin tones?
Absolutely! This glossy berry shade complements a wide range of skin tones, from deep to light (tested across the spectrum from 6 to 1 on the Fitzpatrick scale).
