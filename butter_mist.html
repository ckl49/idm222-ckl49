Frequently Asked Questions

Can Like Butter Mist be used all over the body?

Yes! Like Butter Mist can be used on all external parts of the body. If you're looking to improve skin dryness on the face - we recommend Like Butter Moisturizer.

What is the shelf life of Like Butter Mist?

The shelf life of the Like Butter Mist is 9 months opened and 18 months unopened.

Is Like Butter Mist recyclable?

The components of Like Butter Mist are recyclable but the packaging is plastic mixed materials - pull apart the product in order to recycle; the cap is fully recyclable.

Is Like Butter Mist an aerosol spray?

No, it is not! We are mindful of the harmful impact aerosol sprays have on the environment.

Is Like Butter Mist available at Sephora.com?

Yes!

What does Like Butter Mist smell like?

The Like Butter Mist smells like a blend of plant extracts.

Is Like Butter Mist vegan and gluten free?

Yes! Like Butter Mist is vegan and gluten free.

Where does Like Butter Mist fit into my skin care routine?

We recommend using the mists before thicker serums, after cleansing as the 1st step. Let dry before using moisturizer.
