Frequently Asked Questions

Does Faded Mist bleach the skin?
No, Faded Mist includes ingredients that brighten excess discoloration, but will not lighten normal skin tone. While ingredients like hydroquinone work by killing the cells that produce pigmentation and melanin, the ingredients in Faded Mist like arbutin, licorice root, and tranexamic acid inhibit the enzymes that stimulate pigment-producing cells.
Can the Faded Mist be used all over the body?
Yes! Faded Mist can be used on all external parts of the body. If you're dealing with hyperpigmentation on the face - it's recommended you use the Faded Serum.
