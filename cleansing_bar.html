Frequently Asked Questions

What does Faded Cleansing Bar smell like?

No, it doesn't smell like Faded Serum. Faded Cleansing Bar is fragrance-free, but has a slight shea butter scent. It was designed to be gentle enough for all skin types and daily use. It gently cleanses your skin, leaving you fresh and ready to layer your favorite fragrance.

Is this drying? How does it feel after a shower?

Faded Bar is not drying. Even after rinsing off, the skin should feel cleansed, but soft and supple, never stripped.

Will this work on dry, sensitive, or acne-prone skin?

This product was formulated with all skin types in mind, from dry to sensitive and acne-prone. The rich hydration provided by Shea Butter provides nourishment while the papaya enzyme and kojic acid fade post-acne dark spots.

Do I need to use another cleanser/soap with Faded Bar?

Since this cleansing bar can both cleanse the skin and target body discoloration, you can comfortably skip the other steps and use this product solo.

Which body parts can I use this on?

While this product was designed for use all over ie, armpits, and inner thighs, and face we caution against use on genitalia. For those with more sensitive skin, we recommend you use Faded Cleansing Bar primarily on the body.
