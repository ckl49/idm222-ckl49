Frequently Asked Questions

What is the High Roller?

High Roller is the sure bet to soothe shaving irritation and prevent bumps from ingrown hairs. Hygienic, easy-to use, mess-free package makes treating ingrown hair concerns a breeze. The ultimate jackpot.

What does High Roller do?

High Roller helps prevent future and soothe existing ingrown hairs. Works to reduce razor burn and discomfort related to ingrown hairs. Exfoliates, softens and soothes skin. Helps reverse and prevent discoloration associated with ingrown hairs.

When does High Roller expire?

High Roller does not have an expiration date but should be used within 12 months of opening!

Does High Roller contain any chemicals or fragrances?

High Roller is alcohol-free, fragrance-free, paraben-free, no synthetic dyes. It contains exfoliating, brightening, softening, moisturizing and anti-bacterial ingredients for a multi-benefit cocktail that addresses a range of concerns.

Where should I use High Roller?

High Roller can be used on your neck, underarm, bikini area, legs and can be used by men, women…. Everyone.

How often can I use High Roller?

High Roller should be used 2-3x a week and 1x a day starting out and can be increased to up to 2x a day assuming there is no irritation.
