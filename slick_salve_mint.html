Frequently Asked Questions

What does Slick Salve contain fragrance?
Slick Salve is fragrance-free. It does contain natural mint extract that gives off a scent on its own.
What does Slick Salve taste/smell like?
This balm has a sweet, mint flavor. The scent is slightly minty but subtle.
Is this tinted? What does it look like on the skin?
Slick Salve is not tinted but provides a high-shine gloss. It will boost the appearance of any color when placed on top, leaving the lips looking lustrous and supple.
Does this give a tingly feeling?
No, Slick Salve does not contain any ingredients that would sensitize the lips or make them tingle.
Will this break me out?
There are no ingredients in this product that should lead to breakouts. If you are experiencing this, please discontinue usage.
Does this plump the lips?
No, Slick Salve does not plump the lips. However, it provides deep hydration that may lead to the appearance of fuller lips.
Can I use Slick Salve with lipstick and/or matte lipstick?
Yes, Slick Salve can be used before or after cosmetic lip product application.
