Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Faded Eye Mask with other Topicals products?

Absolutely! For stubborn discoloration, we recommend you follow up with Faded Serum.

Can Faded Eye Mask treat under eye darkness?

If the darkness is caused by skin discoloration and not hollowness, Faded Eye Mask can help reduce the appearance of that discoloration. If the darkness is caused by genetic dark circles, we'd recommend consulting your dermatologist first.

How do I store Faded Eye Mask?

You can store the unopened packettes under most conditions, but we encourage avoiding extreme heat or cold. For a super cooling, Refreshing and depuffing experience, you can store them in the fridge prior to use.

How do I apply Faded Eye Mask?

Apply to clean under eye area for 15 minutes. Use 2-3 times a week. For stubborn discoloration, follow up with Faded Serum. For an extra cool treat, refrigerate the patches prior to use. TIP: To maximize results. Apply the mask in reverse.
